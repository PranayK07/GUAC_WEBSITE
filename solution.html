Our approach to the problem at hand

We have detailed the importance of solving this air pollution problem. Even the smallest dent in this problem can help the overall cause to find a permenant solution.

Our filter will be placed in urban areas with two main placements within high density cities (such as New York) and high emmision zones (such as near a factory). However, approval will be needed from the cities for the placement of our filter within their city. This can be achieved by getting enough signatures on our petition.

You can find the filter at the bottom in the footer

Components of the filter:






HVAC System:

This layer cleans out dust particles in the system that may interrupt the magnesium oxide layer above as well as other chemicals in the air such as methane. This helps improve the function of the upper layers as well as purify the air.

Magnesium Oxide Filter:

This layer utilizes a reaction between carbon dioxide and magnesium oxide to capture the carbon. This removes carbon dioxide from the air that negatively affects urban and suburban communities.

One Way Valve:

This is a layer that prevents air from going out of the filter, but allows it to go upwards. By restricting the airflow running through the filter, we make the filter much more effective.

Drying Layer:

This layer dries the air by utilizing desiccants to remove water vapor in the air. This makes it much easier for chemical process in higher layers to occur, such as the magnesium oxide filter at the top.
