Pollution has become, without a doubt, a very large concern for the future of the planet we love to call Earth.

Greenhouse gasses have caused global temperatures to rise, causing the cryosphere to be affected; ice caps and sheets beginning to melt away.

As a result of the aforementioned melting ice caps, the salinty of the ocean water has also been changing, causing different ocean life to have difficulty thriving.

Project Outline:

Our air filter has been carefully thought out and planned well. We have all worked together to innovate such a creation and have begun attempts to implement this filter into locations with a large greenhouse gas output. We explored new directives of the engineering process to manufacture a design for our users that is made with unique ingenuity. Some of our planning and modelling included the following:

Using a 3D modelling service, such as Blender, our group was able to create a 3D model of the filter that we plan to implement into cities. We started off with a simple 2D blueprint and continued to build on our ideas to form the model which can be viewed here: 3D Model. Having a 3D model is very important for taking things a step further in the engineering process; this model helps give us a quick visual idea of what our filter should look like in-person.

To ensure our information was correct and aspired for complete accuracy when it came to implementing our filter into the real world, we cross checked our work with as many sources as we could and double-checked those sources as well to make sure they were reliable. This is an important part of the project as if you do not check what information you recieve is correct, your project could turn out to be a fail and not work in the real world.

An important part of this science fair is to actually prove to the judges that your innovative design is, not only realistic, but also efficient enough to be implemented into the appropriate environment. It is also important to have everything planned out beforehand, so not just short-term, but also long-term, the events you expect to happen within the upcoming time and how this time will be utilized. We have complimented this important step and our plans can be viewed on this page: Long-Term Plans
